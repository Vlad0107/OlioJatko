"Policies are good when you have complex project and you want to consistent behavior of classes
in the whole system. If you find a bug in your policy, the fix is applied to all host classes without 
further effort. Additionally, if you have a project that needs to configured differently in
various setups, policies are great."

Esim. Erilasiten kielten käyttö. Ohjelma toimii täysin samalla tavalla, mutta riippuen policysta käyttökieli vaihtuu.
